sparrow

Facts

Our incredible sparrow facts have to start with their ease of viewing!
Sparrows are one of the easiest birds to attract to your backyard.
Birders can encourage visits by putting out well-placed feeders.
They’re even responsive to pishing.
Pishing is a way of imitating a bird’s call to attract them.
It’s a useful technique for birders to master.
If the weather is good and there aren’t nearby humans, it’s easy to call for sparrows.
Other birds that respond to pishing include warblers, wrens, kinglets, jays, finches, and chickadees.
A sparrow is a member of the genus Passer.
They are small passerine birds which belong to the family Passeridae.
